Suggestions for giving feedback

  • What is accessibility?
  • Class, race/ethnicity, gender, sexuality, physical/cognitive ability and more... more here
    • financial accessibility: how much does your game cost? what kind of equipment do you need to access it? race/ethnicity: what language is your game available in? race/ethnicity, gender, and sexuality: what kind of representation is in your game? what kind of content warnings might you include to be sensitive to trauma? physical/cognitive: how well versed in game playing do your players need to be? what kind of physical/cognitive faculties do your players need to enjoy the game comfortably?
  • What does the player need to have access in order to play and enjoy your game
