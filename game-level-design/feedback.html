Suggestions for giving feedback

  • Consider asking first:
    • Is there anything specific you are looking for feedback on?
      • It's very possible that the person you're giving feedback for is looking for something specific, or that they didn't have time to get to something.
  • Start by mentioning something you liked!
    • Remember that sharing work can be a vulnerable experience.
