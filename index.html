Who am I?

  • Mickey Sanchez (he/they)
  • Independent game developer
    • Started a game studio called New Beings
    • We've released 2 paid, and 11 small free games since 2015
    • Most recently released a game called Robot Island in June 2020
  • Professional programmer
    • Worked at agencies, a company, and for the last 2 years I've been a freelancer
    • I've done a lot of web development for money, and also made a few small games for money
  • Artist
    • Received a grant from the Portland Institute for Contemporary Art in 2019 to produce an art show and online publication called PIDZN Club, which highlights artists creating with the Asian diaspora
    • I have a Masters in Performance Studies, where I focused mainly on the history of performance art in New York City
  • I have some sound and video editing experience : )

Who I am not:

  • I haven't worked at a large game company.
  • I am not an expert in 2D or 3D visual art and asset creation.
  • I haven't taught a semester long class before!
